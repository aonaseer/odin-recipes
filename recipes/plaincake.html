Plain Cake Recipe

Description

Once you make a cake, you bake for the rest of your life. A cake is a basic skill every person should know. A plain cake is the best choice for a beginner and for a crowd. The cake can then be customised as per one's wish.

Ingredients

  • Self Raising Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • Butter (cooking oil will also do)
  • Caster Sugar (regualar sugar also works)
  • Milk
  • Vanilla food flavour (You can use another food flavour if you wish such as orange flavour)
